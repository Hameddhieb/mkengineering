Presentation

From Building Permit To Final Acceptance

MK ENGINEERING is located in Sfax, Tunisia and intervenes in all project phases from design approval to commissioning and final acceptance, with strong expertise in electricity, fire safety, photovoltaics, energy audit, and energy management.

The office delivers complete studies, tender files, shop drawings, and site monitoring for industrial, tertiary, public, and residential developments in Tunisia and internationally.

Engineering Deliverables
